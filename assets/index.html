IT Without Frontiers IT Without Frontiers
Web Development

IT Without Frontiers was briefed to become the next challenger news portal for the ICT community - competing directly with ITNewsAfrica and ITWeb. Having a set standard as MIT Tech and Popular Mechanics.

The project consisted out of:
  • Competitor Research
  • Brainstorming & Concept Development
  • Final Brand Development
  • Web Development
  • Testing and soft-launch
  • Official Launch

All while this was happening the content was being prepared to suit a certain tone-of-voice and level of relevance for those who would be subscribing. The target market was defined to top-educated individuals who prefer quality over quantity, as ICT news portals often have irrelevant content displayed which broadens their readership statistics, yet fails to retain the valuable readers.

Within the first month of the soft-launch to a dedicated external mailer list of 8K. The mailer campaign was the primary method for exposure and through the soft-launch achieved a 76% click-through rate with an immediate online subscription CTA of 31%.
